About Us

Who We Are

CarePlus is a forward-thinking hygiene products company focused on sustainability, health, and community impact. Our team is dedicated to creating eco-friendly, effective, and affordable solutions for personal and public hygiene.

Mission

To deliver hygiene solutions that improve lives and protect the planet, one product at a time.

Vision

A cleaner, healthier world where everyone has access to essential hygiene tools.

Values

Innovation, Integrity, Sustainability, and Community — these guide every decision we make.

Our Journey

2018 – CarePlus founded with a vision to transform hygiene.
2020 – Launched first eco-friendly wipes line.
2022 – Reached 1 million customers across 10+ countries.
2024 – Partnered with HealthAid Foundation to distribute hygiene kits globally.
